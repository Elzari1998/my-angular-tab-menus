Security

Two-factor authentication (2FA)
We'll prompt your Workspace members to enable 2FA on their next login. To see who has enabled 2FA, check the People settings page.
Single sign-on (SSO)
Require Workspace members to log in to Cagan using their credentials from your SSO identity provider(IDP). Click on your vendor or use SAML to connect with other providers to start the setup process.

Learn more about SSO.
Session management
Set the session and idle timeout durations for your workspace. Members in your workspace will be prompted to log back in after their session expires. Session limits don't apply when using Mobile app.

Learn more about session management.
